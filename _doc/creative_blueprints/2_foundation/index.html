The Problem

Pharmacies are blind to two critical risks — until it's too late

Expired stock on the shelf

Drugs past their expiry date stay in circulation undetected — creating patient safety incidents and serious regulatory liability.

Prescription record gaps

Manual, fragmented records lead to duplicate dispensing, missing documentation, and errors with no audit trail to fall back on.

Reactive, not preventive

Issues are discovered after the fact — when a patient complains, a regulator visits, or stock has already expired on the shelf.

The Solution

PharmaTrack — prevention-first pharmacy operations

Expiry alerts 30 days ahead

Colour-coded dashboards surface near-expiry batches before they become incidents — giving time to rotate, return, or discount.

Digital prescription records

Every prescription is logged, validated against live inventory, and tracked through its full lifecycle — no duplicates, complete audit trail.

Live inventory visibility

Real-time stock levels with reorder alerts ensure the right drugs are always available — and nothing overstays its shelf life.
