# Product Overview — PharmaTrack

## What it is
PharmaTrack is an operational web app for pharmacy owners and their staff to manage drug inventory, track expiry dates, and maintain prescription records — with the primary goals of preventing expired stock from reaching patients and ensuring every prescription is filled accurately.

## Primary Users
- **Pharmacy owners** — oversight, stock decisions, compliance accountability
- **Pharmacy staff / dispensers** — day-to-day stock handling, prescription fulfilment

## Core Problem
Pharmacies running manual or fragmented inventory processes face two compounding risks:
1. **Expired stock** — drugs remaining on shelves past their expiry date, creating patient safety risk and regulatory liability
2. **Prescription inaccuracy** — dispensing errors caused by poor record-keeping, duplicate fulfilments, or missing prescription validation

## Core Solution
A single, always-current operational dashboard where pharmacy owners and staff can:
- Track every drug in stock with quantities and locations
- Monitor expiry dates with automated alerts before stock expires
- Log and verify prescriptions to prevent duplicate dispensing and dispensing errors

## Key Features

### Drug Inventory Management
- Add, update, and remove drugs from stock
- Track current quantity per drug SKU/batch
- Set reorder thresholds with low-stock alerts
- Support batch/lot number tracking for traceability

### Expiry Date Tracking
- Record expiry dates per batch at intake
- Automated colour-coded expiry warnings (e.g. 30-day, 7-day thresholds)
- Dashboard view sorted by soonest-to-expire
- Mark expired stock for removal/return

### Prescription Records
- Log prescriptions with patient reference, drug, dosage, prescribing doctor, and date
- Validate prescriptions against current inventory before dispensing
- Mark prescriptions as filled / partially filled / pending
- Prevent duplicate dispensing of the same prescription

## Strategic Principles
- **Prevention over correction** — surface risks (expiry, low stock, unfilled prescriptions) before they become incidents
- **Simplicity for busy operators** — pharmacy staff are not software specialists; the interface must be scan-able and action-oriented
- **Audit-ready records** — every dispensing action is logged with a timestamp for regulatory review

## Brand & Tone
- **Brand name**: PharmaTrack
- **Tone**: Calm, precise, trustworthy — clinical clarity without jargon
- **Visual direction**: Clean whites and deep blues/greens; professional, medical-grade aesthetic

## Scope (MVP)
Web app. Three primary modules: Inventory, Expiry Tracker, Prescriptions. Intended for single-location pharmacies scaling to small chains.
